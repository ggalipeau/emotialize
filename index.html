How to Use

  • Make sure you accept the Webcam prompt from your browser
  • Emotialize takes pictures when the progress bar is finshed based face detection
  • The images are processed for emotions
  • The aggregates are shown in the chart area
  • You can view your data by Daily or Hourly charts
  • You can take a manual picture by choosing the camera
    • Automated pictures will stop while in camera mode
    • Choose 'Continue Auto Capture' to start automated pictures again
    • Hover over your face to see that images emotions in camera mode
